# Martin Watzinger - Academic Website

This repository contains the personal academic website for Professor Martin Watzinger, hosted on GitHub Pages at [https://martinwatzinger.github.io/website/](https://martinwatzinger.github.io/website/).

## About

Martin Watzinger is a Professor of Economics at the University of Münster and a research affiliate at CEPR and CESIfo. He is also a Director at the Münster Center for Economic Policy and a Research Professor at the REACH EUREGIO Start-up Center.

His research focuses on:
- The relation of competition and innovation
- The role of science for the innovation process
